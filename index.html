Most of the systems we live with weren't built to be fair or humane. They were built to work at scale and sort themselves out later. We're living in the later.

Evening Build is for people who work in and around tech and want to think more clearly about what we're doing. Designers, engineers, PMs, ops people, data folks, API tinkerers. People who care about how things get made and who they leave behind.

It's also for anyone outside that bubble who still feels the impact. Whether you're trying to fix a login form for your neighbor or wondering why your bus never shows up on time, you're not imagining it. A lot of this stuff really is broken.

If you're interested in what care looks like after the hype wears off, you're in the right place.
