Evening Build

A dinner club for people building things—projects, careers, habits, worlds.

We gather every so often to share food and conversation with others who are in the middle of something, figuring something out, or quietly shaping something new.

It's not a show-and-tell, and it's not a networking thing. It's a place to practice being around other people who care.

Come with something you've been chewing on—literal or metaphorical.
